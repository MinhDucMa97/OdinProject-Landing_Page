My Favorite One Piece Characters
Monkey D.Luffy is the determined and rubber-bodied protagonist of "One Piece" on a quest to become the Pirate King and find the legendary treasure, One Piece.
Whitebeard, a fearsome pirate and one of the Four Emperors in "One Piece", possessed increbidble power through his earthquake-inducing Devil Fruit abilities.
Zoro is a swordsman from "One Piece", aspiring to be the best, with a distinct three-sword technique.
Sakazuki, also known as Akainu, is a powerful Marine Admiral in "One Piece", recognized for his ruthless pursuit of justice and his magma-based Devil Fruit powers.
